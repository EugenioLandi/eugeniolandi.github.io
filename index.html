Eugenio Landi


About me:

I am currently a postdoc at Università degli studi di Roma “La Sapienza”, starting in June 2023.

I obtained my Ph.D. in mathematics at Università di Roma Tre under the supervision of professor Domenico Fiorenza.
My research interests are algebraic topology and higher category theory.
For my Ph.D. thesis, titled "Equivariant localization methods, orientations and modularity", I worked on orientations, T-duality, rational homotopy theory, TQFTs and equivariant cohomology.

You can email me at: eugenio.landi@uniroma1.it.
